Porsche Taycan Poster Design

I wanted to create a poster that is emblematic of the bold move by Porsche toward electric vehicles with the introduction of the Taycan in 2018. The combination of the ferociousness of the headlights and elegant body lines symbolic of Porsche brings the brand into a new frontier.
